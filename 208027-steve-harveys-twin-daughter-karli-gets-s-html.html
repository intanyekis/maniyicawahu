Steve Harvey's Twin Daughter Karli Gets Special Mother's Day Tribute from Husband Ben Raymond

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 12, 2024

Steve Harvey's daughter Karli gets a sweet Mother's Day tribute from her husband Ben Raymond who prepared a simple surprise at home for her to enjoy.

Karli Harvey-Raymond got a special Mother's Day tribute from her husband Ben, who filled a table in their home with blooming flowers and cards made just for her. On top of this, he shared an Instagram story with a picture of Karli, calling her a "queen."

In 2016, Karli and Ben welcomed their baby boy, Benjamin II. While they acknowledged that they hoped to celebrate married life with traveling and expensive dinners first, they were surprised to have received the blessing of a child early.

STEVE'S SUPPORT FOR KARLI

Although Karli's all grown up, her dad Steve has always been hands-on, even when it came to preparing for the arrival of his grandson. At the time, he joined the planning sessions for creating the baby's nursery, hiring his own interior designer to help them with it.

Throughout the quarantine period, Steve and Marjorie Harvey have been keeping themselves busy at home.

Ben Raymond's Mother's Day tribute for Karli | Source: Instagram/iambenraymond

Years later, the family remains tight-knit despite not being able to see each other because of the stay-at-home orders in place. For now, Karli and Ben are enjoying life at home with their special bundle of joy and that's enough for them.

A picture of Karli Harvey posted by her husband Ben on Instagram | Source: Instagram/iambenraymond

THE HARVEYS IN QUARANTINE

For some members of the Harvey family, however, the COVID-19 pandemic is a bit too much to bear. For their other grandson Ezra in particular, he shares he hasn't been okay, and his grandmother made sure to resonate with him.

"Ezra.... I understand baby... we all have questions. #quarantine #mood #grandson"

THEIR QUARANTINE WOES

Ezra Harvey is the youngest child of Jason Harvey and is also the youngest grandchild of the famous couple. He was born on September 5, 2018, and even at such a young age, he is already feeling the stay-at-home woes.

Throughout the quarantine period, Steve and Marjorie Harvey have been keeping themselves busy at home. Although this has resulted in getting in each other's nerves at times, the pair still proves to be a great team and has made it work nonetheless.

During this time that they can't see the rest of their family, they've been using social media to keep in touch, and it's a sacrifice they've been willing to make to ensure their safety.
