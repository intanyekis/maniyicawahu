How Old Is Mumbo Jumbo? Find Him On Instagram

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, May 22, 2024

Famed YouTuber Mumbo Jumbo is 25 years old. Here is more about him.

Oliver Brotherhood, aka Mumbo Jumbo, is a Minecraft Youtuber. He is best known for showcasing his Redstone builds and testing new features. Moreover, he is a member of the Hermitcraft SMP server in Minecraft.

Speaking a bit about his career, he was approached to join Hermicraft. Since then, he is one of the most active members. Also, he is interested in filmmaking as he has introduced a short film named ‘Snow in the Forest‘ with several other series. Moreover, he also owns a production house called 2Story.

Mumbo Jumbo Age And Real Name Revealed

Mumbo Jumbo is 25 years old as of June 2021. He was born in Farnham in the year 1995 and used to celebrate his birthday on December 1st every year,

The real name of the YouTuber is Oliver Brotherhood. However, he is popularly known as Mumbo Jumbo on the stage.

Find Him On Instagram

Mumbo Jumbo is active on Instagram under the username @officialmumbo. 

In his official IG account, he has got more than 239k followers. Till now, he has shared more than eight hundred posts among his followers. 

Also, he is present on Twitter under the username @ThatMumboJumbo. He has got more than 351k followers.

Mumbo Jumbo Youtube Career 

Brotherhood created the MumboJumbo Youtube channel on March 30th, 2012, and started uploading videos from the next day.

Mumbo’s first video on youtube was ‘ServerCraft Survival: Episode 5: To The Nether’. Initially, he started being a ‘Viva’ as he was known as Viva on Call of Duty. He started the Youtube channel with his friend Tom, but his friend left the channel.

Currently, Oliver is the owner of the Youtube Channel named Mumbo Jumbo, which has over 7.5 million subscribers. Also, he has got another Youtube channel TheMumbo in which he has got 21k subscribers.
