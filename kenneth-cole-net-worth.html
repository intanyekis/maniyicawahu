Early Life and Education

Kenneth Cole, a renowned fashion designer and entrepreneur, was born on March 23, 1954, in Brooklyn, New York City. Coming from a Jewish family, his upbringing played a significant role in shaping his passion for creativity and innovation.

During his formative years, Cole attended John L. Miller Great Neck North High School, where he discovered his flair for design and fashion. He continued his education at Emory College of Arts and Sciences, a prestigious institution located in Atlanta, Georgia. In 1976, he graduated with a degree that laid a strong foundation for his future endeavors in the fashion industry.

Kenneth Cole Productions
