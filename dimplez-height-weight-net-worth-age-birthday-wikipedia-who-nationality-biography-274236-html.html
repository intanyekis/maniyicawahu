Dimplez is a Nigerian American music executive who currently sits as the SVP of Digital Marketing & Content Development for Capitol Music Group.

She is also the founder of her very own marketing consultancy firm, IJEOMA.

Her success stems from her previous career positions at Capitol Records, Motown Records, Universal Music Group, BWR PR, and Uproxx. In the past at Capitol Music Group, Dimplez was responsible for urban digital marketing efforts across Capitol Records and Motown Records.
