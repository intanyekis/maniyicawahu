Nikki Bella &amp; The Young Bucks Matt Jackson Interact Over All In Total Divas

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 26, 2024

WWE is using a familiar phrase to hype the upcoming eighth season of the E! hit reality series Total Divas: All In.

The #TotalDivas cast is ALL IN for Season 8, premiering Sept. 19 at 9/8c on @e_entertainment! https://t.co/8A8qQR9VA1@BellaTwins @NiaJaxWWE @RealPaigeWWE @LanaWWE @NaomiWWE @NatbyNature pic.twitter.com/Zs3mBZbUK6

— WWE (@WWE) July 26, 2018

All In happens to be the name of Cody Rhodes & The Young Bucks’ upcoming September 1st wrestling event in Chicago, IL.

After Total Divas star Nikki Bella retweeted the above video, Matt Jackson of The Young Bucks responded with the following Cody Rhodes inspired GIF:

https://twitter.com/MattJackson13/status/1022505267873832961

Nikki Bella then replied to Matt Jackson with the following:

Lol we all felt the same when we saw it too! ? N

— Nikki & Brie (@BellaTwins) July 26, 2018

When Nikki Bella responded with the words, “We felt the same when we saw it too,” she may have been referring to the Total Divas cast mates she tagged in the tweet; Nia Jax, Paige, Lana, Naomi, and Natalya.

Matt Jackson wrapped it all up with an emoticon:

https://twitter.com/MattJackson13/status/1022610019731165190
