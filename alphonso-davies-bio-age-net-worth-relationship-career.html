Alphonso Boyle Davies is a Canadian professional soccer player who plays as a left defender or winger for both the Canadian national team and the Bundesliga club Bayern Munich. His speed, dribbling ability, and creativity have made him one of the world’s best full-backs.

Where was Alphonso Davies born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Alphonso Davies was born in a refugee camp in Buduburam, Ghana, as the fourth child of six to Liberian parents who had been uprooted during the Second Liberian Civil War.

After traveling to Edmonton, they finally landed in Canada in 2005. Davies participated in a football league called Free Footie, which is for elementary school kids who cannot afford uniforms, game tickets, or transportation.

Following that, he signed up for the soccer accelerator program at University Hill Secondary School and began playing with the Edmonton Internationals and Edmonton Strikers.

He started attending the Whitecaps FC Academy at the age of 14. In 2016, Davies broke the record for the youngest player in the USL when he signed with Whitecaps FC 2. Making his squad debut in April 2016, he scored his first goal on May 15, 2016, making him the youngest goal scorer in USL history. He held this record until the year 2020.
