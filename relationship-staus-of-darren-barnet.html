Who is Darren Barnet Dating? His Love Life

Who is Mikaela Hoover?

Mikaela Hoover was born on July 12, 1984, in Colbert, Washington, United States. She graduated from Loyola Marymount University with her bachelor's degree in the theatre program in Los Angeles.

In 2007, she kicked off her acting career with the film, Frank. Since then, she has starred in several TV shows such as Happy Endings, 2 Broke Girls,  How I Met Your Mother, Lucifer Airplane Mode, and The Suicide Squad. 

Also, she played the character of Chelsea in Netflix romantic comedy Love Hard, with Darren in 2021.
