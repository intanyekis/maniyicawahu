How Old Is Julia Garner? When Did The American Actress Come Into Limelight?

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, May 22, 2024

Julia Garner is one of the most compelling actresses after being in the limelight in the Netflix series Ozark, where she plays the role of Ruth Langmore. 

The fans were impressed by her performance and got curious about her age and where she belongs.

Julia Garner was born on 1 February 1994 in New York City, US. She completed her graduation from Indiana University. Currently, the young actress is 27 years old and posted a picture on Instagram on her birthday with the caption, ‘happy 27th birthday’.

Everything You Need To Know About Julia Garner

In her early life, Julia’s father, Thomas Garner, was a painter and an art teacher by profession, whereas her mother, Tami Gingold, was a therapist by profession. Moreover, her mother was famous for her acting and comedy in her native Israel.

Julia also has a sibling, her sister named Anna Garner, who is three years older than her and an artist and teacher at a school in manhattan. 

At the age of 15, Julia decided to overcome her fear of being introverted all the time by enrolling in dance lessons. Julia explains how she was involved in school production before being driven into acting. 

She said, “I took acting classes for a hobby because I was very shy. I was 15 and I wanted to keep myself busy. I really liked how it felt.”

After making her first film, she shared her experiences, saying, “Every film or production or TV show you always learn something from it but you don’t really know what you’re learning until after you shoot it.”

She didn’t get to know about the business after the first film, but she understood how she had to work and behave on the set. 

Julia Acting In Short Films

She also said in an interview that when she was in Columbia grad school, she would participate in short films. 

At 16, she did her first experience in a short film, The Dreamer, the same year, and she reappeared in a short film, One Thousand Cranes. 

At 17, she was finally in the line of acting by being introduced to the world by Suan Durkin’s movie Martha Marcy May Marlene. 

She explained her experience with Martha Marcy May Marlene, and she says, “Then I did this student film – I used to do a lot of student films for Columbia Film School when I was 15, and the student filmmaker’s girlfriend was interning at a casting agency.

“So my first audition was for an open casting call, which was thousands of people and I didn’t get the job. It was me and another girl.”

The other girl was not finalized but, after a few days, Julia received a call that she had been confirmed for Martha Marcy May Marlene. 

In an interview, she said that although she didn’t know anyone in the film industry, her role helped her to move at a pace in her career. 

In 2012, David Chase invited her to portray a role in his movie Not Fade Away, and that role was specially written for Julia. 

Julia began moving along as her career spiked by her incredible acting performance in movies such as Electrick Children(2012) and The Last Exorcism Part II etc. 

She made her debut in tv shows in 2015 by The Americans. She was also featured in Girls in 2016, the same year she was enrolled for the Netflix series, The Get Down.

At 24, she appeared in lead roles in Everything Is Beautiful Far Away, Tomatoes Red, and One Percent More Humid. 

Julia’s Best Role

Then came the turning point of her career; in 2017, she was enrolled in the Ozark series for playing the role of Ruth Langmore. 

All the viewers loved this Netflix series. The star went on to win the 2017 Emmy awards for her outstanding performance, and she won another Emmy award for Ruth later on.

After playing Ruth Langmore for multiple series, Julia gave her thoughts, “I love it. You don’t want the same arc. I feel my characters are not characters; I see them as people.”

She emphasized Ruth, saying, “You can’t really judge them. And I love that [the writers] change her all the time. Well, I don’t want to say ‘change’ because every year, people evolve, good or bad.”

Just like fans, Julia considers the turning point to be Ruth. Although she played a role in movies and series, including short films, her hype built up after Ruth. 

Julia’s Marriage 

In April 2019, Julia was engaged to Mark Foster. They both dated for ten months until they decided to tie the knot and get married to each other in December 2019. 

Her husband is a musician, a songwriter, and the lead singer in his band Foster the People. So far up till now, the band has only released three albums and fifteen music videos.

What Was Next For Julia After Ruth?

After playing the role of Ruth Langmore, Julia gained a major boost in her career. She signed a project with Waco comedy-drama for which she appeared in 6 episodes of the comedy series. 

Then, she was seen in five episodes of Maniac, which was also a comedy-drama. This wasn’t it; in 2019, she appeared in two episodes of Modern Love, released on amazon prime video. 

In 2020, it was speculated that Julia would be featured in the new upcoming Netflix series 2020. But, things didn’t proceed as it was planned, and the first trailer of her new Netflix series named “Inventing Anna” was released on 22 November 2021.

Starring the lead actor, Julia, a real-life scammer who defrauded wealthy New Yorkers by posing as a German heiress. The fans are teased with the trailer and just waiting for it to be on air.
