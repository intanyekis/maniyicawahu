Ben Richardson Dating! Bagged Anna Kendrick As Girlfriend, 'Pitch Perfect' Couple

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

British cinematographer, Ben Richardson had a knack for staying behind the camera, but his romantic involvement with famous girlfriend brought him in front of the screens this time.

Ben Richardson is widely known for his feature films, Beasts of the Southern Wild and The Fault in Our Stars where he handled all the cinematography.

Height Of Movie-Making: Ben Richardson talks about his future projects and experience on finding out director Steven Spielberg is a big fan of his work (Published on 25 February 2013)

Ben received Best Cinematography prizes at the 2012 Sundance Film Festival and the 2013 Independent Spirit Awards for his work on the Oscar-nominated, Beasts of the Southern Wild.

Ben Richardson’s Bio: Age & Movie Career

Born on 21 September 1983, Benjamin T. Richardson was raised in England, United Kingdom. He attended Royal Holloway, University of London and studied media arts degree programme.

The 34-year-old Brit was initially hired for as the cinematographer for Beasts of the Southern Wild for only the small portions. But as the producers saw his shot on a test reel, they wasted no time in hiring him on shooting the entire film.

Ben is also responsible for shooting the 2014 summer hit The Fault In Our Stars which was based on John Green’s best-selling novel. His work, Seed won Best Animated Short at the 2010 Slamdance Film Festival.

He has also done collaborations with director Joe Swanberg that include Happy Christmas starring Anna Kendrick and Melanie Lynskey and Drinking Buddies with Olivia Wilde and Jake Johnson.

Ben’s recent projects include Sand Castle, which is set during the 2003 Iraq occupation, Table 19, and Taylor Sheridan’s Wind River all in 2017.

Ben Richardson’s Flattering Net Worth

Having worked for different movies that made millions of money, Ben has undoubtedly made more than an average cinematographer.

His famous works, The Fault In Our Stars and Beasts of the Southern Wild especially brought millions to the door and Ben certainly earned a nasty amount of money from those.

Ben Richardson Is Dating Pitch Perfect Star, Anna Kendrick

While residing behind the camera, capturing the moment from the best angle, Ben has removed his single tag and has been dating his superstar girlfriend, Anna Kendrick since 2014.

Ben met Anna during the shooting of the 2013 comedy, Drinking Buddies, where Anna starred alongside Olivia Wilde and Ben, was filming the scenes.

And if anyone’s wondering why their relationship wasn’t all over the headlines during that time, it’s because both Ben and Anna are privacy seekers and they had it secured under the wraps until the paparazzi uncovered the secret.

It was in 2015 that the two were frequently spotted together and there were several pictures of the pair over the internet.
