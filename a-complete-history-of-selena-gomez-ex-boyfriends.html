A Complete History Of Selena Gomez's Ex-Boyfriends

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, May 20, 2024

Highlights

  • Selena Gomez has had a high-profile dating history, with relationships with Nick Jonas and Taylor Lautner, to name a few.
  • Many of Gomez's relationships have been publicized, but she may be trying to keep her romantic life more private now.
  • Gomez has had mixed experiences with her past relationships, describing some as "sweet" and others as "damaging," but she seems to be embracing her single lifestyle for now.

Selena Gomez’s dating life has been a subject of public speculation for over a decade. The former Disney star has had a number of high-profile relationships and been linked to many famous men over the years. But you may be surprised to know all of the gentlemen she’s been involved with.

Many of Gomez's relationships have remained rumors, but some were confirmed after the fact. After dating men like Justin Bieber and The Weeknd very publicly, Gomez may be attempting to keep her romantic life a little more private. Below, we take a look at Selena’s complete dating history from beginning to start.

Related: How Selena Gomez Is Adding To Her Current Net Worth

11 Nick Jonas

Before he was married to Priyanka Chopra, Nick Jonas had a brief relationship with Selena Gomez in 2008. At the time, the two were both Disney stars. She even appeared as Nick’s on-screen love interest in the music video for “Burnin’ Up.”

In 2015, Selena addressed their time together, describing it as “puppy love.” “It was very sweet,” the singer continued. "He's lovely. I do have a lot of love for him, for sure."

10 Taylor Lautner

The year after her fling with Nick, Selena moved on with Twilight star Taylor Lautner, who had just been thrust into international stardom for his portrayal of Jacob Black in the hit franchise.

Years later, Selena explained that she met Taylor when Kristen Stewart was staying in her hotel. However, she admitted the media attention became too much for them to handle. “People were getting a little crazy about us,” she said. Lautner is now married to Taylor Dome, and their relationship seems very sweet.

9 Orlando Bloom

Orlando Bloom is now engaged to Katy Perry, but he was briefly involved with Selena in 2014 when the paparazzi spotted them spending time together. That same year, the Lord of the Rings actor allegedly threw a punch at Justin Bieber after an unexpected run-in, leading to speculation that Justin was bothered by Orlando's relationship with Selena.

In 2016, it was rumored that Orlando and Selena were back together after photos showed them getting close in Las Vegas. However, the actor was publicly with Katy at the time, who went on to refer to the rumors as a “dumb conspiracy.” Selena then re-tweeted Katy’s quote.

8 Niall Horan

Selena appeared to have gotten herself a One Direction member in 2015 when she was spotted hanging out with Niall Horan on multiple occasions. They were even spotted getting physical at Jenna Dewan’s 35th birthday party. Though rumors suggested they were rekindling things in 2019, Niall quickly told the media he was single. Since then, the two haven’t been linked to each other.

7 Zedd

After collaborating on music, Zedd and Selena Gomez hit it off in early 2015. The relationship appeared to fizzle out naturally, though the Wizards of Waverly Place alum later said “I adore Zedd” when asked about their time together.

Zedd has also opened up about the experience, but he admits it was challenging due to paparazzi attention. The DJ says people were even trying to hack his family’s phones to get intel on his relationship with Selena.

6 Samuel Krost

Following her fling with Zedd, Selena found romance with Samuel Kross from late 2015 to 2016, who’s now best known for being involved in the infamous Fyre Festival. Despite their short-lived romance, Samuel has had only sweet things to say about his ex. “Selena is a real girl with a real heart and real feelings,” he previously said on social media. “All you superficial who are just unhappy with your love lives, understand that love is real," he continued. "Although we have ventured on separate paths, at least I know that love exists. I love you, Sel.”

The two were spotted hanging out in 2021 in New York, but it seems nothing came of their one-on-one time.

5 Charlie Puth

Selena clearly has a thing for fellow musicians. After collaborating on the song “We Don’t Talk Anymore” in 2016, Selena reportedly became involved in Charlie Puth. The romance was short-lived, but Charlie has been vocal about how damaging their time together was for him.

In 2018, Charlie admitted their relationship was “short-lived” but very “impactful” and that it “messed me up.” “I’m trying to put this the best way possible: It wasn’t like I was the only person on her mind,” the singer explained. “And I think I knew that going in — what I was getting myself into.”

4 The Weeknd

Only months after his break-up from Bella Hadid, The Weeknd began dating Selena in early 2017. The pair made their red carpet debut at the Met Gala and he was apparently supportive during Selena’s health struggles.

However, by the end of the year, photos showed Selena hanging out with her on-and-off boyfriend, Justin Bieber, making it clear she’d broken up with The Weeknd. He went on to rekindle his romance with Bella, though they’ve since parted ways.

Related: Who Are The Weeknd's Biggest Songs About?

3 Justin Bieber

Arguably, Selena’s most high-profile relationship was with Justin Bieber. The duo was on-and-off for nearly a decade before Justin tied the knot with Hailey Baldwin in 2018. Justin and Selena were first linked in 2011. Though they had multiple break-ups and reconciliations, they ultimately called things off for good the same year Justin married Hailey.

Selena has made it clear she doesn’t have positive feelings when reflecting on their time together, accusing Justin of “emotional abuse” in 2020.

2 Nat Wolff

During the 2022 Fourth of July Weekend, Selena was spotted spending time with former Nickelodeon star Nat Wolff, sparking dating rumors.

The two haven't commented on their relationship status, but they’ve known each other for years, having both started in the entertainment industry as children around the same time. They even starred in 2014’s Behaving Badly together.

1 Andrea Iervolino

Most recently, Selena is rumored to be involved with Italian film producer, Andrea Iervolino. The pair sparked dating rumors after they were spotted vacationing in Italy together in August 2022. However, the two have apparently known each other for years, as they first sparked romance rumors in 2019 after Andrea attended Selena’s 27th birthday.

Although it’s unclear if Selena is in a relationship or not, the singer is still waiting on her happily ever after. Her newest single 'Single Soon' suggests that the pop star is enjoying the single lifestyle, and fans are loving that for her.
