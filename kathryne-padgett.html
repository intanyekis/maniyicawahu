Her Age &amp; Net Worth

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 12, 2024

Kathryne Padgett is an American fitness influencer and model who came to recognition after the rumors of her being the girlfriend of Alex Rodriguez came around. She is an online health and wellness coach famous for her diet, weight loss tips, and health-related videos.

Kathryne also goes by the nickname "Kat." After being associated with Alex, she became quite a hot topic among the public. So you must have probably heard about her. Besides being a fitness model, she is also an NPC competitor. Moreover, she is also an entrepreneur and a former soccer player.

Kat's Recent Rumored Breakup With Alex Rodriguez Blew Up The Internet

Kathryne Padgett and Alex Rodriguez have been rumored to be dating since the beginning of this year. Though the couple never confirmed the news, their relationship was obvious enough to know that they were dating. Also, an insider revealed that they are indeed dating but casually.

And now the news of Kat and Alex breaking up has surfaced and swept the entire industry. This news was also revealed by an insider and not the personalities themselves.

The source stated that Kat and A-Rod are now good friends. They said:

“They’re good friends – they’re just each single. They broke up, but they’re super tight. It’s all good. Alex is having fun and hanging out with his family now."

However, before Kat dated A-Rod, she was in a relationship with Tyler Sheppard. He is a chemical engineer who is an alumnus of Lamar University. Her first recorded relationship was with Houston Robert.

Kat's Relationship Timeline With A-Rod

In January this year, Alex Rodriguez, also known as A-Rod and Kathryne Padgett, made headlines after they were spotted together at Lambeau Field. They were seen watching the Green Bay Packers vs. San Francisco 49ers game. After they were seen cheering the teams together, rumors of them dating sprung up and covered the entire industry.

Soon after, Kat and A-Rod were seen at Minnesota Timberwolves game. This only fired their dating rumors. However, neither of the party confirmed the news of their dating. Then in June, they went on a vacation to Italy. They were spotted kissing, dancing, and having fun during the night out in Italy.

Later, A-Rod and Kat were seen walking hand in hand, leaving Machine Gun Kelly's after-party that was held at Catch Steak following his concert at Madison Square Garden Tuesday night. Though they have not talked about their relationship, an insider revealed that they both are casually dating.

Has Kat Moved On From A-Rod?

As per what social media of Kat suggests, she has already moved on from Alex Rodriguez. She posted her pictures with a guy dressing up for Halloween. However, the man had his face covered in a mask. 

Though Kat hasn't said anything about dating the guy, she wrote a statement on her social media, presumingly for her previous relationship with A-Rod. Her caption in that post was: 

 Dating a villain is better than dating a fake hero.             

Net Worth: How Rich Is Kathryne?

The ex-girlfriend of Alex Rodriguez, Kat, is the Director of Nutrition at Doyle Sports Performance. She has been doing great in her career as a dietitian and nutrition expert. So she is sure to have been bagging quite a good sum of money.

The net worth of Kathryne Padgett is estimated to be around $1 million. As a popular fitness influencer, she is also a model for a number of fashion and activewear brands like Vital Apparel. She is also involved with the fitness company named Alchemy Labs.

Physical Appearance: How Tall Is Kathryne? 

This will surprise you all, but our gorgeous fitness influencer Kat is just 5 feet 3 inches (160 cm) tall. As a fitness guru, she looks tall, posing in her gym clothes and lifting those heavy plates. However, she stands at an average height, contrary to what we thought. Regarding her weight, she weighs around 55 kg.

Kat is blonde with beautiful ocean-blue-colored eyes. Since she is in fitness, she has a toned and a little muscular build. Her body frame and features also come off as strong.

Kat's Early Life & Family Background

Kathryne Padgett is from the United States, born in Beaumont, Texas. However, she currently resides in Dallas. She was born into a typical Christian family, and she is of white descent. 

Kat's father is James Padgett, whereas her mother is Stephanie Padgett. According to sources, her father is an attorney who works for a local law firm in Texas. She was brought up in a family of seven with four siblings.

Has Four Siblings 

As aforementioned, Kat has four siblings with whom she shares a good bonding. But you'll be surprised to learn that the bodybuilder's siblings are also professional athletes with their own distinct areas of expertise.

Raegan Padgett and Kenedy Padgett are Kat's sisters, whereas Blain Padgett and Grayson Padgett are her brothers. Though her siblings, Blain and Kenedy, do not have much information on them, her other siblings are athletes and have their own profiles in their respective fields.

Kat's brother, Grayson, is a baseball player in OF position and plays for the University of Houston. He is also on a baseball roster and has already achieved many feats. On the other hand, their sister, Raegan, is a soccer player who plays for Baylor.

Kat Played For Her College's Basketball Team

According to Kat's social media, she had been pursuing her RD (Registered Dietitian) degree. It seems that she has obtained her degree. However, before that, she went to the University of St. Thomas and completed her bachelor's degree there.

Kat is also a soccer player who played for the University of St. Thomas while she was there. While in college, she was a part of the women's soccer team and played as a member throughout her time there. Moreover, she also received the "Champions of Character" award for her contributions in 2018.

Kat's Sister Raegan Is Engaged To Shea Langeliers

Though you must have been familiar with Kathryne Padgett after learning about her relationship with A-rod, you might not have bothered to look into her life. But did you know that the fitness influencer's younger sister, Raegan Padgett, is engaged with another baseball player, Shea Langeliers?

Kat's sister, Raegan, and the Oakland Athletics catcher, Shea Lageliers, got engaged in January this year. According to sportskeeda, the couple was already together for four years before finally getting engaged.

Kat's Fitness Career

Kat started working on her fitness career as soon as she graduated from her college, realizing her interests. She made her debut as a bodybuilder in 2021 at the NPC Branch Warren Classic in Texas. In the bikini competition, Kat ranked eighth in category C. Moreover, she also won Wellness in the same category.

Though there's no exact timeline, Kat started working with numerous popular fitness magazines soon after her NPC debut. She started working with many apparel brands and fitness products. Currently, she is working as the Director of Nutrition at Doyle Sports Performance.

Social Media Influence

Being a fitness and lifestyle influencer, Kathryne has a considerable influence as a personality in such a field. So, she keeps making frequent posts and is pretty active on social media.

On Instagram, she is available under the username @katpadgettfit and has over 37.7K followers. However, it seems she is not available on Twitter.
