Who Won Battle on the Beach Tonight? 2023 Weekly Winners

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Team Alison Victoria won Battle on the Beach Bedroom 2023. Ben Argall and Pete Meldrum are the winners of Battle on the Beach bedroom challenge.

Season three of Battle on the Beach premiered on June 4, 2023, at 9:00 p.m. exclusively on HGTV channel.

Battle on the Beach 2023 cast includes Ty Pennington, Alison Victoria, and Taniya Nayak as a mentor and two super judges Bryan and Sarah Baeumbler.

Alison Victoria’s team consists of Benjamin Argall and Pete Meldrum and team Tanya consists of newly married couple Kelsey and Ryan Mansingh.

The judges and the mentors of the show are regulars on the HGTV channel. Previously they are been part of Rock The Block, Renovation Island, and Ty Breakers.

The mentors have to guide the other two contestants who will be on the team and compete against each other in the weekly challenges.

Each team has a budget of $90,000 and they have to manage everything from that money until the end.

Who Won Battle on the Beach Tonight?

Alison Victoria team won the Battle on the Beach bedroom challenge this week. Her team includes Ben Argall and Pete Meldrum.

Team Ty Pennington won the first episode, Kitchen Challenge on June 5, 2023.

Battle on the Beach Season 2 episode 2 was named Main Suite Mayhem. Each team has to renovate the highest-value main bedroom and bathroom.

The second challenge is won by Team Alison as they outdo the other contestant and renovate the bedroom and bathroom perfectly.

The fans of the show were happy to see Team Alson winning the second episode. The fans even praise her team members.

Battle on the Beach season 2 episode 1 was named Kitchen Knockdown. Each team has to renovate the kitchen on a tight budget.

Unlike last season, this time the houses are not identical and have various different features and have an extra 200 square feet.

Each team gets a house comprising two-bedroom, two-bath, and kitchen seaside properties in Gulf Shores, Alabama.

The teams which perform exceptionally well renovating the building till the end will be eligible to win the cash prize of $50,000.

The fans of the show were super excited about the renewal of the show. Everyone congratulated Ty’s team for their first win.

The third episode of the reality show Battle on the Beach will air on June 18, 2023, on the HGTV channel.

Battle On The Beach Weekly Winners

The HGTV reality show consists of a total of six episodes, the episode will air every Sunday at 9:00 p.m. The second episode will be available on June 11.

The first episode was a two-hour-long special episode, the first challenge is completed and the viewers are eager to know about the second challenge.

As the show progresses, we will be able to see the best out of every contestant, and also everyone is excited to see the title winners.

With the little twist and turns, the format of the show seems to be the same as the previous Battle on the Beach seasons.

As per the show format, the winner of the competition show is only one team, but the team can prove their worth every week and gain the value of their property by being weekly winners.

Episode 1: Kitchen Knockdown

In season 1, the first episode was won by team Alison with team members Sunjay and Darryl.

In Season 2, Team Victoria which includes Paige and Corey Cyr of Ft. McMurray, Alberta, Canada won this challenge.

Battle on the Beach Season 3 kitchen winner of the first week is Ty Pennington and his team. The episode premiered on June 4, 2023.

Episode 2: Main Suite Mayhem

In Season 1 of the HGTV show, Team Pennington along with his team member Sunny and Josh won the Main Suit weekly challenge.

Team Pennington won the Main Suite Mayhem weekly challenge in season 2. Team members are Wally Remaley and Jacqueline Matoza.

Episode 3: Living Room Showdown

The season 1, Living Room Showdown weekly challenge was won by team Tanya Nayak with her team member Kerry and David.

In season 2, the winner of this challenge was Team Alison along with her team member.

Episode 4: Kids Room Rivalry

The winner of the Kids Room battle challenge was Sunny and Josh from Team Pennington in season 1.

The father and son duo Roosevelt and Brandyn Chambers of St. Louis, Missouri mentored by Tanya Nayak won this challenge in season 2.

Episode 5: Guest Suite Glow Up

In Season 2, the winner of this weekly challenge was Team Pennington with his two other team members.

Battle on the Beach Season 1 and 2 Winners

Battle on the Beach winners are Tanya Nayak and Alison Victoria for season 1 and season 2, respectively.

The first season was judged by Holmes and Holmes Star. Similar to this year, the teams were divided into pair and had to work together.

In season one, each team had a budget of $75000 to use for their project. The winning team was proving themselves every week.

Fans were happy to see Kerry Harvick and her husband David Kersh winning the competition and bagging the prize money as a winning prize.

Their design included a children’s pier-themed room as well as a tiled bathroom that included a vanity made of salvaged wood.

Their unique idea includes a hanging bed which reflects their ability to transform a room and make it unusually beautiful.

The couple also owned a real estate firm named Kersh Real Estate which is located in Texas.

Similarly, the season two winning couple belonged to Mentor Alison Victoria’s team named Fort McMurray and Paige Cyr.

The couple are real estate agents and were able to take the winning prize all by themselves with the guidance of their mentor.

They were able to increase the value of the property by renovating the house. It was raised more than $200 thousand dollar than the original price.

The previous season can be watched on FuboTV, Max, HBO Max, Amazon Channel, Discovert Plus, DIRECTV, Roku, and Sling Tv.
