Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 240: Release Date, Spoilers, and What to Expect

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, July 28, 2024

Jujutsu Kaisen is one of the world’s most popular manga series, with over 60 million copies in print. Yuji Itadori, a high school student, is drawn into a hidden fight between jujutsu sorcerers and cursed spirits.

Gege Akutami wrote and drew the manga, which has been serialized in Weekly Shonen Jump since March 2018. The manga is currently in its 11th arc, titled Shinjuku Showdown Arc, and involves a climactic showdown between the King of Curses, Sukuna, and the jujutsu sorcerer, Satoru Gojo.

The most recent chapter, 239, concluded with an unexpected showdown between Kenjaku, the series’s main antagonist, and Fumihiko Takaba, a strange individual who appears to be immune to Kenjaku’s attacks.

Fans are looking forward to the next chapter, 240, to see how this fight plays out and what Kenjaku’s final strategy is. This page contains information about Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 240, such as the release date, spoilers, and what to expect.

Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 240 Release Date

Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 240 is scheduled for release on October 29, 2023. The manga is published on a weekly basis, with new chapters released every Sunday on Viz Media’s website and Shueisha’s Manga Plus app.

Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 240 Spoilers

There are no official spoilers or raw images for Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 240 as of yet. They do, however, generally leak online a few days before the actual release date. Fans can stay current on spoilers and raw scans by visiting various online forums and social media sites. We recommend that readers wait for the official release and support the inventor.

What to Expect in Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 240?

Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 240 will most likely focus on Kenjaku and Takaba’s duel, revealing more about their origins and talents. Kenjaku is the commander of the Culling Game, a deadly competition in which competitors slaughter one another in order to get points and live longer.

He is also the original user of Noritoshi Kamo’s body and has possessed a number of jujutsu sorcerers throughout history. Takaba is an enigmatic character who first appears in Chapter 239.

He resembles Takako Uro, a jujutsu sorcerer from thousands of years ago who was engaged in the invention of the Prison Realm, a particular barrier capable of sealing anybody inside it. Takaba claims to be Takako Uro’s reincarnation rather than hers.

He also appears to harbor a vendetta toward Kenjaku, challenging him to a battle. Kenjaku and Takaba’s fight will most likely reveal more about their motivations and aims. Kenjaku has declared that he wishes to abolish the Culling Game and join mankind with Tengen, the god-like creature who guards Japan against foreign curses.

His exact motives, however, remain unknown. Takaba, who appears to know Kenjaku from a previous life, may have some insight into his ambitions. Meanwhile, fans are curious about the outcome of Sukuna and Gojo’s battle, which was halted by Kenjaku’s intervention.

Sukuna, the most powerful cursed ghost in existence, inhabits Yuji’s body. Gojo is the world’s strongest jujutsu sorcerer, possessing the Six Eyes and the Limitless skills. Their confrontation was billed as the ultimate battle between the two most powerful forces in the Jujutsu Kaisen cosmos.

Kenjaku, however, utilized his cursed manipulation skill to take possession of Gojo’s body and confine him inside the prison realm before they could conclude their fight. Sukuna was enraged and irritated because he wanted to settle his score with Gojo once and for all. Fans are speculating whether Sukuna will attempt to rescue Gojo from the Prison Realm or pursue Kenjaku instead.

Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 240 should clarify some of these questions while also providing more exciting action and plot twists. Fans may look forward to reading it on Viz Media or Manga Plus on October 29th.
