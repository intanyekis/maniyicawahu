Former Actor, Ian Nene Shares &quot;Illuminati&quot; Photos, Citing New Religion (Photos)

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Former Machachari actor, Ian Nene is most definitely raising eyebrows, posing hard questions to Kenyans and fans are wondering, “Warrathooose”!

The young lad earlier declared that he is on a quest to find himself but many now believe that he is past the quest of finding himself and is just sharing who he is now.

Ian, famously known as Almasi shared photos of himself doing “satanic” poses and fans are just left scratching their heads questioning if he has joined the illuminati.

He captioned the photo using a line from the recent Avengers movie, Infinity war.

“As a person puts on new garments, giving up old ones, the soul similarly accepts new material bodies, giving up the old and useless ones” reads the caption in part.
