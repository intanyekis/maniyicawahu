Naming a cat can be a lot of fun, but it can also be intimidating to pick the perfect name, especially when you are thinking outside the box. The good news for spooky enthusiasts is that there are tons of great name options related to Halloween and everything spooky season. If you’re struggling to pick the perfect name for your Halloween cat, keep reading for the best name ideas!

How to Name Your Spooky Cat

Naming your cat after something Halloween-related can be a little overwhelming just because there are so many wonderful names to choose from. To narrow your options, start by identifying what type of name you’re looking for. Decide if you are hoping for something cute that makes you smile every time you say it or are you feeling something scary that will strike fear into your cat’s enemies.

If your cat is black or orange, there are lots of names that relate directly to your cat’s color. Your cat’s personality traits can also be matched up with a character from a horror story. Maybe you’re thinking of a name that relates to your favorite author? Narrow the field by selecting a category you’re most interested in and see if you find the perfect choices on our list of 200 names. If not, branch further out!
