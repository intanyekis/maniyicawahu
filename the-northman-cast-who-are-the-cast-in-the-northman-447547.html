The Northman Cast 2022, Who Are The Cast In The Northman?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

The Northman

Robert Eggers co-wrote the screenplay with Sjón for The Northman, a 2022 American epic historical fiction film directed by Robert Eggers. The film stars co-producer Alexander Skarsgard, Nicole Kidman, Claes Bang, Anya Taylor-Joy, Ethan Hawke, Björk, and Willem Dafoe and is based on the legend of Amleth. The story follows Amleth, a Viking prince who embarks on a journey to get revenge on his father's murder. After meeting to discuss future collaborations, Skarsgard expressed interest in making a Viking film, and Eggers decided to make it his third project. From August to December 2020, filming took place in sites across Northern Ireland and Iceland, with the majority of the actors joining in October 2019.
