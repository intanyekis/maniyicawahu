Who Is Gina-Maria Schumacher? Everything To Know About F1 Racer Mick Schumacher

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

 Born to sports celebrity dad, Gina-Maria Schumacher, 24, is also pacing towards establishing her name through sports, but a different one from her dad’s.

Gina-Maria Schumacher is the daughter of the German Formula One legend Michael Schumacher.

While her father, uncle, and brother shine as F1 racing drivers, she earns herself sports stardom through horse-riding and ranks 7th in the FEI Reining Seniors Ranking.

Gina-Maria Schumacher Wikipedia And Age Details

Unlike all of her family members, 24 years old Gina-Maria Schumacher is not available on Wikipedia yet.

Born on February 20, 1997, one might have assumed that she would walk the same path as her dad when she kart raced for a season.

However, she chose Western riding that her mom liked most and did her best in that sector.

Further, Gina is the winner of the 2017 FEI World Reining Championship held in Switzerland.

Gina-Maria Schumacher Parents: Daughter Of Michael Schumacher

Gina-Maria Schumacher is daughter of her popular parents Michael Schumacher and Corinna Betsch Schumacher.

Her dad, Michael, is a former Formula 1 racing driver who competed for Jordan, Ferrari, Benetton, and Mercedes.

The seven-time Formula 1 World Champion retired in 2012, holding the most winning record of 91 among various other works.

Likewise, Gina’s mom Corinna, is a horsewoman, an animal rights activist, and a skilled horserider. Her mother also won the 2010 European Championship in western style horse riding.

At the age of 52 years, the Schumacher couple has been married since August 1995 and owns horse ranches in Texas and Switzerland.

Recently, Michael Schumacher’s 3-decades-long journey has been presented in a sports documentary film Schumacher premiering on September 15 on Netflix.

Gina-Maria Schumacher Brother: Mick Schumacher Sister

Gina-Maria Schumacher has a younger brother Mick Schumacher who is also a professional racing driver.

The 22-year-old Swiss-born German sportsman’s motorsport career started in 2008 under the name Mick Betsch to avoid the attention from his dad’s fame.

From driving his dad’s championship-winning cars to being a member at Ferrari Driver Academy, Mick has progressed to Formula 1, advancing through F4, F3, and F2.

After his father Michael and uncle Ralf, he is the third Schumacher to race in Formula One as he made his debut in the 2021 season with the Haas F1 Team.

Further, both brother Mick and sister Gina-Maria excel at their sports and show immense support and love to each other.

Gina-Maria Schumacher Boyfriend And Instagram

Gina-Maria Schumacher is dating her long-term boyfriend, Iain Bethke, and little does she show him off on her Instagram profile.

Iain is a show jumper and is seen alongside Gina cheering in most of her races. However, like the whole Schumacher family, she also likes to keep her private life concealed to herself.

On Instagram, Gina-Maria goes by the username @gina_schumacher among her 91.8k followers.

In 482 of her posts, she mostly shares pictures with her horses, occasionally bringing up her father and brother.
