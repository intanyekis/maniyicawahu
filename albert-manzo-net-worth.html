Albert Manzo has an estimated net worth of $12 million. Albert is an American television personality who is best known for his appearances on the popular and controversial television show, “The Real Housewives of New Jersey”. His wealth primarily comes from this show.

Albert stars in this show along with his wife, Caroline Manzo. Caroline’s sister Dina Manzo is also a part of this show. in this show, Caroline and Albert has stressed on their Italian heritage and also spoken about the devotion to their family. Dina has also been speaking on those lines.

Interestingly, Albert’s father also had the same name – Albert (Tiny) Manzo. Tiny, as he was popularly called, was killed in true mob-style in 1983, when it was suspected that he along with Peter A. Campisi, the family soldier of the Gambino’s, were busy skimming from one mob casino. They were executed on Staten Island. The cops were never able to solve the murder.

Albert Manzo is also the co-owner of “The Brownstone”, which is an event and catering service. Much of Albert’s wealth comes from this business too. He lives in New Jersey. His first appearance on the show was in the year 2009.

The couple has three children, Albert “Albie” Manzo IV who was born in the year 1986, Lauren Michele Manzo who was born in 1988, and Christopher Thomas Manzo who was born in 1989. It has been said that Albert and Carolina are going stable.
